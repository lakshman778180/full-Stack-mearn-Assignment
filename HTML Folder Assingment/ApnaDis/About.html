About of jananki Mandir

Janaki Mandir is a famous Hindu temple in Janakpur, Nepal, dedicated to Goddess Sita (Janaki), the consort of Lord Rama. It is one of the most significant pilgrimage sites for Hindus, especially those who follow the Ramayana.
