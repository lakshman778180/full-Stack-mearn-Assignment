About Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, India, is a stunning mausoleum built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife Mumtaz Mahal.

History

Construction of the Taj Mahal began in 1632 and took approximately 22 years to complete. It is recognized as a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
